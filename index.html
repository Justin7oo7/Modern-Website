Rules & Tips

Quick guides to get you playing confidently and courteously.

Break, choose solids or stripes, pocket all of your group, then the 8-ball last. Fouls give ball-in-hand.

Pot reds then colors, scoring per color value; highest score wins the frame. Fouls award points to opponent.

Score by striking both object balls with the cue ball after at least three cushion contacts.

  • Stance: Stable feet, chin low, cue level.
  • Bridge: Solid hand bridge; keep the cue smooth.
  • Aim: Visualize contact points; follow through.
  • Respect shots—stay still and quiet while others play.
  • Chalk away from the cloth; keep drinks off tables.
  • Call shots clearly in casual 8-ball games.
